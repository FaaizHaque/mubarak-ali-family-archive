Notable Members

Members of the family who have served with distinction — in public life, the professions, and in holding the family together. This page grows as members are recognised.

Add a notable member

  1. Find the person in assets/data.js and add notable: true, plus optional role: "…" and honors: "…".
  2. For someone who married into the family (not on the tree), add them to the notableExtra list near the top of assets/data.js.
